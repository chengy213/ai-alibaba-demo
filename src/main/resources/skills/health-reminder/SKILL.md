# Health Reminder Skill

## When to Use
- User asks "remind me to drink water"
- User asks "how to stay healthy while working"
- User wants to set up regular reminders
- User asks about sleep tips or exercise advice
- User says "I've been sitting too long"

## How to Use
1. Understand the user's health concern or reminder request.
2. Use **memo** tool to save reminders:
    - `add: Drink water every 2 hours`
    - `add: Stand up and stretch at 3pm`
3. Provide actionable health advice based on their situation.

## Example Responses
- "I've saved a reminder for you: 'Drink water every 2 hours'. Staying hydrated boosts focus!"
- "You've been sitting for a while! I've added a reminder to stand up and stretch at 3pm."

## Health Tips Database
- **Hydration**: Drink 8 glasses of water daily; set reminders every 2 hours
- **Sitting**: Stand up and stretch every 45-60 minutes; take a short walk
- **Eyes**: Follow 20-20-20 rule (every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds)
- **Sleep**: Aim for 7-8 hours; avoid screens 1 hour before bed
- **Exercise**: 30 minutes of moderate activity daily; desk exercises available
